# Therin Sunscale

Therin is an imposing dragonborn cleric
who recently accompanied the party
as an independent sword-for-hire
contracted to escort a secret caravan to Uthodurn.
Clad in armor that proudly bears
the platinum crest of Bahamut,
he is a devout and brave warrior
who did not hesitate to charge
a deadly, panther-riding assassin
in a desperate attempt
to protect their cargo.
Though the adventurers do not know him very well yet,
they quickly learned
to respect his resilience
after he survived
being struck down
and drained of his life force
by a cursed glaive.
Possessing striking golden eyes and a stalwart demeanor,
Therin has taken solemn responsibility for safeguarding
the meager remnants of their looted transport—
a magical dagger and a strange Aeorian orb.
He now travels onward with his new companions
into the grand, mithril-inlaid gates of Uthodurn,
ready to face the daunting task
of answering for their disastrous expedition.
