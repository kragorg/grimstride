# Doctor Pepe

Doctor Pepe is a pragmatic and stealthy rogue
who initially crossed paths with the party
while investigating the Frigid Woe affliction
in Palebank Village.
A master of lockpicking and striking from the shadows,
he favors his crossbow and daggers,
though his bravery often gives way
to tactical retreats and hiding
during overwhelming encounters.
He possesses a magical Ring of Comprehension
that allows him to read esoteric scripts,
and notably wears a unique two-billed olive drab hat.
Having recently survived near-drowning
in Salsvault
and squandered his expedition riches
on hedonistic pursuits with a dwarven pelt-sharer,
he travels onward with the party
toward Uthodurn, ever on the lookout
for a lucrative score
and a safe hiding spot.
