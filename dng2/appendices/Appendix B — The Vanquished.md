# Appendix B — The Vanquished

1.  **The Daemon (Luddenbrock Mines, Kaspien’s Laboratory)** — *Doctor Pepe* delivered the killing blow, driving his blade into the creature with surgical finality. The daemon had been restrained by *Scarlet’s* Entangle; struck three times with radiant energy by *Kragor’s* magic longsword; pierced by *Halite’s* trident; twice struck by *Elara’s* enchanted arrows; scorched by *Therin’s* burning hands and then seared by his Radiance of the Dawn; gashed by *Whisper’s* claws; and mauled twice by *Scarlet* in Moorbounder form. The killing was set up by *Kragor*, who—on death’s door—cast *Armor of Agathys* and deliberately offered himself as bait; the daemon bit down and the detonating ice armor blasted cold damage into its face and throat.

2.  **The Ruby Golem (Luddenbrock Mines, Kaspien’s Laboratory)** — *Scarlet* stopped the golem by seizing its command amulet while in rat form. The golem now answers to Scarlet.

3.  **Kaspien Vohlkrist, Lich (Luddenbrock Mines, Kaspien’s Laboratory)** — *Kragor* destroyed the lich, accidentally, by reaching for the fractured crystal vial with his spectral hand; the glass snapped in two, the soul-spark dissipated, and the eight-hundred-year-old body crumbled instantly to dust.

4.  **Flea, Homunculus (Luddenbrock Mines, Kaspien’s Laboratory)** — Ceased to exist the instant *Kragor* shattered Kaspien’s soul-vessel; with its master’s essence gone, its tether to the mortal plane snapped and the creature simply vanished.

5.  **Frost Giant, Greataxe (The Mountain Pass)** — *Hilda Brackmar* delivered the killing blow, opening the giant's throat with her battleaxe and driving a second stroke into its chest. The giant had been wounded by *Kragor's* Eldritch Blast and psychic daggers; pierced by crossbow bolts from *Doctor Pepe* and *Brennik*; slashed by the echoes of *Aedric* and *Vornesh*; struck by *Elara's* magic missiles; and mauled by *Hilda's* axe in the initial exchange.

6.  **Frost Giantess, Greatsword (The Mountain Pass)** — Executed by *Elara* with three streaks of force to the back of the head. The giantess had been forced to grovel by *Elara's* command; carved by the echoes of *Vornesh* and *Aedric*; hacked by *Hilda's* battleaxe; struck by crossbow bolts from *Doctor Pepe* and *Brennik*; wounded by *Kragor's* Eldritch Blast; weakened by *Therin's* necrotic tolling; scorched by *Serath's* holy radiance; and battered by his spectral mace.

7.  **Roper, the First (The Underdark, Faerzress caverns)** — *Therin* finished it with a searing ray, the stony hide sagging and cracking apart in the violet light. It had been battered down by *Elara's* repeated magic missiles; mauled by *Scarlet* in moorbounder form; savaged by her summoned puma, *Tiny*; and ground away by *Kragor's* teleported Cloud of Daggers.

8.  **Roper, the Second (The Underdark, Faerzress caverns)** — *Vornesh* killed it with a thrown spear that punched clean through the joint of its body. It had been struck by crossbow bolts from *Doctor Pepe* and *Brennik*; torn by *Scarlet* in moorbounder form; battered by *Serath's* spectral mace; and whittled down by *Kragor's* teleported Cloud of Daggers.

9.  **Roper, the Eldest (The Underdark, Faerzress caverns)** — *Therin* ended it with a mote of Sacred Flame, the last roper coming apart in radiant fire. The largest of the three, it had been opened three times over by *Vornesh's* poisoned scimitars; struck by bolts from *Brennik* and *Doctor Pepe*; pierced by *Elara's* arrows; cursed by *Serath's* Bane and lit by *Therin's* Faerie Fire; and ground down by *Kragor's* drifting daggers as it grappled the party in its dying spasm.
