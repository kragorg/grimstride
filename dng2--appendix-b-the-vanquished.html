Appendix B — The Vanquished

  1. The Daemon (Luddenbrock Mines, Kaspien’s Laboratory) — Doctor Pepe delivered the killing blow, driving his blade into the creature with surgical finality. The daemon had been restrained by Scarlet’s Entangle; struck three times with radiant energy by Kragor’s magic longsword; pierced by Halite’s trident; twice struck by Elara’s enchanted arrows; scorched by Therin’s burning hands and then seared by his Radiance of the Dawn; gashed by Whisper’s claws; and mauled twice by Scarlet in Moorbounder form. The killing was set up by Kragor, who—on death’s door—cast Armor of Agathys and deliberately offered himself as bait; the daemon bit down and the detonating ice armor blasted cold damage into its face and throat.

  2. The Ruby Golem (Luddenbrock Mines, Kaspien’s Laboratory) — Scarlet stopped the golem by seizing its command amulet while in rat form. The golem now answers to Scarlet.

  3. Kaspien Vohlkrist, Lich (Luddenbrock Mines, Kaspien’s Laboratory) — Kragor destroyed the lich, accidentally, by reaching for the fractured crystal vial with his spectral hand; the glass snapped in two, the soul-spark dissipated, and the eight-hundred-year-old body crumbled instantly to dust.

  4. Flea, Homunculus (Luddenbrock Mines, Kaspien’s Laboratory) — Ceased to exist the instant Kragor shattered Kaspien’s soul-vessel; with its master’s essence gone, its tether to the mortal plane snapped and the creature simply vanished.
